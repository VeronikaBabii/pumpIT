Junior iOS Developer

Home Positions Junior iOS Developer

If you do not let your mobile phone out of your hands and want to understand the principle of how the programs that you use work and believe that the future belongs to mobile technologies - the specialty of an iOS developer will be interesting for you, since it requires some creativity and deep practical training on mobile programming.

iOS developer is a programmer who develops and maintains software for the iOS operating system. Using the programming language Objective-C or Swift, you can create mobile applications for the most popular iOS operating system.

The iOS developer also bears part of the responsibility for the rating of a mobile application, since the clearer the interface and functionality, the more appropriate way the data is structured, the more time the user will devote to the application.

Who is iOS developer?

This is a programmer who develops and maintains applications for iOS - an operating system created by Apple for its own mobile devices.

Main activities:

- development of the interface and functionality of programs for mobile devices using iOS;
- creation of technical documentation for developed programs;
- debugging and testing applications;
- downloading the developed software to the App Store;
- support and updating of existing applications.

Requirements for iOS developer:

- Knowledge of Swift / Objective-C;
- Knowledge of basic design patterns (MVC / MVVM / MVP / VIPER and others);
- Good understanding of basic frameworks (UIKit, Foundation and others);
- Knowledge of basic development patterns (Singelton, Observer, Delegate and others);
- Ability to analyze, study, master and apply new technologies;
- English at a level not lower than Intermediate.
