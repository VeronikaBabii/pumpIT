iOS Developer Profession

  • 16th October, 2020
  • Unknown

In today's world, the profession of programmer is becoming increasingly popular. A programmer is a person who creates various applications, programs and algorithms using special programming languages. This profession is suitable for those people who are interested in and interested in modern technology, languages and sciences. The profession is suitable for both men and women, age does not matter at all. In order to be successful in programming, you need good enough skills and abilities, and this requires patience to learn. In programming it is impossible to do without logical and abstract thinking, analytical skills, as well as deep and detailed knowledge in the field of information technology.

There is a great variety of programmers

They are mainly divided into the following:

  • Applied programmers. They mainly develop games, Apple programming, messengers, accounting programs, various editors, as well as create software for fire extinguishing systems, fire alarms, audio and video surveillance systems, security, etc. They often deal with adapting existing programs to specific needs.

  • System programmers. They develop operating systems, write interfaces to a variety of databases, and work with the network. Specialists working in this field are the highest paid, and they are, in fact, very few, because not everyone, even a good programmer, can be perfectly versed in this type of programming. Their task is to develop software that will manage the entire system and ensure its proper operation and functioning.

  • Web programmers. They mainly work with the network - the Internet. They create programs that are the pantries of web pages, create the pages themselves, the interface, etc.

Requirements for programmers:

Programming is an area that is actively developing and therefore has a number of requirements for those who would like to master it. The programmer must have the ability to quickly adapt to all modern technologies and constantly master new ones. The ability to self-study is one of the main features that a programmer should have. Another important criterion is proficiency in English, especially when reading technical documentation. A very important factor is the ability to work as a team on large projects.

Salary of a programmer in Ukraine

In the last few years, the salaries of programmers in Ukraine have tended to increase and are quite high. On average, a Ukrainian programmer earns about $ 5,000 a month. The average monthly salary of a senior developer for the latter has grown by $ 180, Middle has grown by $ 300, and Junior only by $ 50. In Kyiv, a developer of the Junior level can receive $ 750, while in Dnipro the average salary of a programmer of the same level is $ 600. Dnipro also has a low salary level for Senior developers, which is $ 3,400, as opposed to $ 3,900 in Kyiv and $ 3,500 in Lviv and Kharkiv. In smaller cities, such as Ivano-Frankivsk, the salary of the same developer is a little more than $ 2000.

Swift programming

Today, the programming language and Swift courses are becoming increasingly popular. This language was developed by Aple. With Swift you can write applications for mobile applications, desktop, servers, etc. The language has its own Playground, in which you can immediately see the result of programming. Swift has special libraries that are designed to develop various applications for iPhone, iPad and MacBook. The big advantage of Swift is the speed of code execution and program operation. Swift is 2.5 times faster than Objective-C and 8 times faster than Python 2.7. Also, this programming language is famous for its conciseness and easy to read. Reading the code is not difficult, even for those who do not know Swift, but speak any other programming language.

IOS developer

Training in IOS development requires a future programmer to have a higher technical education. If you already have experience in programming in Java or you can try to master IOS-programming, studying the necessary literature, communicating with programmers in specialized forums. If you do not have programming experience, it is better to find appropriate IT courses where the student can learn to work with programming languages, as well as C ++, Swift, SQL, Objective C and all other tools necessary for development.

The development of ios applications today is in high demand. That is why, mastering the profession of a programmer, you can not be afraid to lose your job.
