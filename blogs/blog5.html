What position should you take in the IT sphere?

  • 16th October, 2020
  • Unknown

Advantages of the IT sphere

Almost every day in the modern world new professions appear. With the rapid development of new technologies, IT professions are gaining popularity in many countries among people of all ages. The huge interest in the IT sphere is due to the significant advantages of the profession. Consider the most important:

  • High salaries in IT companies. One of the highest paid sectors of the economy is the IT sector. Every year, its revenues grow, bringing the IT industry to the forefront of the most lucrative professions. Thus, in Ukraine, the field of IT technologies is among the TOP-5 highest paid professions;

  • Relatively little competition in the IT sector. There are not many real professionals in the field, but with the growth and development of technology, the IT field is improving quite rapidly, which will eventually lead to fierce competition. Therefore, today is a very promising time to master the IT profession;

  • Rapid career growth. The IT industry offers huge professional growth for beginners in the IT field and is ready to offer great prospects for the future and work in well-known international companies;

  • Possibility of remote work. Quite a lot of IT workers work remotely, which allows you to be in different countries around the world, feeling your own comfort;

  • Constant professional and personal development. Many companies encourage their employees with useful and interesting courses, learning foreign languages.

Technical professions in IT companies:

The profession of a programmer is one of the main ones in the IT field. It is more prevalent for people with a technical mind, who have a calm balanced character, because the profession requires the ability to spend a lot of time at the computer and a lot of hard work to achieve successful results. The most popular technical professions in the IT field are:

  • Java programmer. The key task is to develop unique and complex applications based on the programming language of the same name, which is used to create video games, Android applications and web applications using JSP;

  • IOS programmer. The main function is to develop software, create applications, video games and interfaces for Apple;

  • Android programmer. Specializes in creating applications, interfaces, optimizing resources for the Android operating system;

  • QA Tester. The task is to control the quality of products that exist in companies. Constantly checks the operation of applications, site and other products for compliance with quality standards;

  • Frontend developer. Engaged in the creation of the client part of the site, design. Must know Java-Script programming language.

Non-technical professions in IT companies:

In order to have a job in the IT field, it is not necessary to have a mathematical mind and know programming languages. The IT industry is so large that it offers many non-technical professions. Consider the main:

  • Project manager. Engaged in the formation and creation of a strategy for the development of the company's products. Is responsible for the work of the project team and must have good abilities to organize the work of the team;

  • HR manager. The main task is to select and adapt employees. Must have clear criteria for selecting staff for certain positions. The professionalism of the team largely depends on his work;

  • Sales manager. The profession is ideal for people with developed communication skills, the ability to persuade and competently offer the company's products. The main task in the IT sphere is to find and establish long-term working relationships with clients of companies;

  • Motion designer. The basis of the profession is the creation of motion graphics, which combines audio, video and text into a single animation complex. A relatively new and promising profession that is gaining momentum recently.

How can you master modern IT professions?

As we mentioned earlier, the professions of the IT sector are divided into technical and non-technical, which means a real opportunity to find a job in the IT sector for almost anyone who dreams of discovering a promising world of the IT industry. However, many people are asked whether it is possible to find a job in it without experience and specialized education? Yes, it is affordable! To date, many specialized courses are offered, but the choice should be treated with caution, because there are many dishonest companies that do not keep their promises to customers.
