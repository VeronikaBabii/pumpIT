How to learn programming online? Tips for beginners

  • 16th October, 2020
  • Unknown

Programming is a fairly routine activity. To master the language you need to process a lot of information. To learn how to program online, it is advisable to follow some simple rules. Then the learning process will be much faster, more interesting and more efficient.

1. Be sure to solve practical problems, study non-standard examples. This will help to quickly understand the essence of the programming language, better understand its internal logic and get rid of monotonous memorization.

2. Communicate more with colleagues, participate in conferences, ask questions on forums.

3. Learn the language with a mentor. Participate in various projects, startups, hackathons.

4. Use training sites. Use a game form of learning.

5. Search for information on the Internet, use free online books.

6. Do not jump to another language, do not learn two programming languages at the same time. First, study one well, master it for confident application in practice. Only then can we move on to study the next.

7. Make a clear learning plan. Understand exactly what you need to learn and in what order.

Conclusion

When studying programming, it is necessary to pay maximum attention to the practical side of the issue. Communication with colleagues is very important. Problem solving, work on real projects, participation in startups, conferences, hackathons and forums - all this will help to master the specialty faster and more efficiently. It is equally important to choose the right course of study and make a clear development plan. Specialized resources will help you with this. The PumpIT site contains a lot of information on programming for beginners. Here you will be able to get acquainted with effective courses on learning programming languages and mastering various professions in IT.
